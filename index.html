About Me

Hey there! I’m Ramakrishna Makani, an Embedded Systems and Robotics enthusiast on a mission to make machines smarter, faster, and a lot more interesting. Armed with a Master’s in Automation and Robotics from TU Dortmund, Germany, I’ve spent 3+ years turning industrial automation challenges into innovative solutions—everything from crafting ultra-lightweight embedded SDKs and smart sensor networks and onto building trust frameworks for humanoid robots (yes, my master’s thesis was a real page-turner!). I thrive in R&D labs, at trade fairs, and anywhere the coffee is strong and the problems are tough. Whether I’m hacking away in C/C++, wrangling microcontrollers, or designing resilient control systems, I love building tech that actually works in the real world. I’ve led teams, designed and planned from research to products, and always bring a dose of curiosity (and a bit of fun) to every project. If you’re looking for someone who gets excited about Industry 4.0, real-time systems, and bringing creative ideas to life—let’s connect!
